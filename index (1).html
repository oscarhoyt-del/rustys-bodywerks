No — and this is important. In Texas, you have the legal right to choose your own repair facility, regardless of what your insurance company tells you. Many insurers will try to steer you toward their "preferred" or "approved" shops — called Direct Repair Program (DRP) shops. Here's what they don't tell you: those shops have a financial agreement with the insurance company to keep repair costs down. That can mean skipping manufacturer-required procedures, using cheaper aftermarket parts instead of factory parts, or bypassing post-repair safety inspections. The result? A car that looks fixed but may not perform the way it was designed to in your next accident — affecting everything from airbag deployment to your vehicle's crash safety rating. At Rusty's Bodywerks, we work with all insurers every day — but we answer to you, not them. We follow every manufacturer repair procedure, use factory-approved parts, and complete all required safety inspections on every vehicle.
